About Us

Philly Phestivities was founded by Alice, Clarissa, Michelle, and Tamera at FemmeHacks 2017.

Our goal was to take all events in the Philadelphia area and put them in one place, so you weekend plans are now at the tips of your fingers. Enjoy!
